ABOUT US

       
       
           

What is V-CLos?

           
               

V-CLos is a 3DCG live production collective, led primarily by students from Senzoku Gakuen College of Music, in pursuit of next-generation entertainment.

Our ambition extends beyond the scope of typical fan-made projects; we strive for a singular virtual experience that fuses the "overwhelming intensity of live instrumentation"—only possible from a college of music—with "state-of-the-art 3DCG technology" harnessed through our in-house motion capture systems.

From planning and performance to sound design, CG motion, and visual direction, every element is crafted by students. We are dedicated to originating innovative forms of expression that we alone can deliver.                

           
       
       
           

What is the VOCA-Fes Series?

           
               

The "VOCA-Fes Series" is the flagship live project from V-CLos, representing the culmination of our collective efforts.

At its core is an "uncompromising dedication to live instrumentation". While many other fan-made productions utilise pre-recorded sound sources, VOCA-Fes generates an electric groove with real-time band performances.

Furthermore, this series is the "crystallisation of our research". Each performance is built around a new theme of "technical challenges", allowing us to break new ground in fan-made live entertainment.                

           
       
       
           

TEAM

           
               

The V-CLos production process is founded upon four specialist sections, pooling expertise from across the college, and a collaborative support structure.

                               
                       
  • [Performance Team]
    Responsible for the live band, manipulators (controlling synchronised audio), and lead sheet production.
    •                    
  • [Sound Team]
    Responsible for sequence production (programming) and vocal tuning.
    •                    
  • [Visual Team]
    Responsible for 3D motion production, background visuals (VJ), and dance capture using the campus motion capture system with student actors.
    •                    
  • [Planning & PR Team]
    Responsible for planning the live events and handling public relations.
    •                
               

In addition to these core V-CLos sections, we have a collaborative framework with external student staff (lighting, audio) to pool campus-wide resources, balancing academic research with professional, practical application.
