ABOUT US

           
               

What is V-CLos?

               

V-CLos is a 3DCG live production collective, led primarily by students from Senzoku Gakuen College of Music, in pursuit of next-generation entertainment.

Our ambition extends beyond the scope of typical fan-made projects; we strive for a singular virtual experience that fuses the "overwhelming intensity of live instrumentation" with "state-of-the-art 3DCG technology".

From planning and performance to visuals, every element is crafted by students. We are originating new forms of fan-made live entertainment, beginning with the "VOCA-Fes Series".
